## 🧭 Task 1: Business Understanding

## Overview

This project was completed as part of the **BCG X Data Science Job Simulation** on **Forage**.

This first task was an onboarding and context-setting step rather than a hands-on deliverable — the goal was to understand BCG X's role, meet the client, and learn the methodology that would frame every task that followed.

---

## What This Task Covered

### Meeting BCG X

BCG X is BCG's tech build and design unit, combining technology, data science, and human-centered design to solve client problems. This task set the scene for working as a **Junior Data Scientist** on a live case team.

### The BCG X 5-Step Data Science Methodology

Every case at BCG X follows the same underlying process:

1. **Business understanding & problem framing** — what is the context of the problem, and why does it matter?
2. **Exploratory data analysis & data cleaning** — what data are we working with, and how can we improve it?
3. **Feature engineering** — can the dataset be enriched with domain expertise or third-party information?
4. **Modeling & evaluation** — can the data reliably predict the outcome we care about?
5. **Insights & recommendations** — how do we communicate the value of these predictions in a way that matters to the business?

This structure underpins Tasks 2 through 6 in this repository.

### The Client Brief: PowerCo

- **PowerCo** is a major gas and electricity utility supplying small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs).
- The energy market has become more competitive, giving customers more choice than ever.
- PowerCo is concerned about **customer churn** — customers switching to other providers — and has engaged BCG X to diagnose the cause.
- One early hypothesis from the Associate Director: churn may be driven by **price sensitivity** — how much price influences a customer's decision to stay or leave.

This hypothesis is what Task 2 goes on to investigate.
